Quiz question:

In which Grand Slam final did Federer beat Rafael Nadal for the first time?

(Hover over for answer)

Answer: Wimbledon 2006

Federer beat Nadal at Wimbledon in 2006 and 2007 but lost to Nadal in the 2008 Wimbledon final. Nadal thus became the first player to beat Roger Federer in a Grand Slam final!
